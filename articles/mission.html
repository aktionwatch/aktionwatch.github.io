Our Mission

At Aktion Watch, we believe in the power of active citizenship to drive local action towards the 17 Sustainable Development Goals. Our organization is dedicated to promoting environmental, cultural, and animal welfare in our community and beyond. We work to empower individuals and organizations to make a positive impact on the world around us through education, advocacy, and action. By working together, we can create a more sustainable and just world for all. Join us in our mission and be a part of the solution!

Aktion Watch, rooted in the passionate efforts of a group of Vonitsa’s citizens, is dedicated to nurturing a harmonious relationship between our community, nature, and all living beings. Born from the union of Stray Smiles, the echoes of our cultural events, and the bonds formed through our town’s twinning program, we stand as a beacon of active citizenship.

Our mission is to safeguard the environmental sanctity of the Vonitsa region, a land rich in natural beauty and cultural heritage. We are committed to the welfare of our region's stray animals, ensuring their wellbeing through treatment and adoption initiatives.

By championing sustainability, education, and solidarity, we engage in activism and social media outreach. Our festivals and events are not just celebrations but powerful catalysts for change, uniting our community in the pursuit of a sustainable and vibrant future.

Aktion Watch envisions a world where active citizenship and cooperative endeavors pave the way for a sustainable tomorrow, where every action echoes our deep respect for nature and all its inhabitants.
